ABOUT ME...

In the year 2024, it all started. (ART) On the beginning of the year, I broke my foot and started spending most of my time at home. Dying of boredom, I started drawing from my the head, until one night, while listening to Jimmy Hendrick's Voodoo Child, I felt an immense energy over me; my hand started moving in a crazy way, almost like I was being possessed. The sketch ended up telling me to stop; my whole body went numb. Feeling really tired, I went to sleep right away. When I woke up, a vivid image of my dream was clear in my head: the biggest sun eclipse I have ever seen. My art completely changed, turned dark, more spiritual than drawings from the head, and I started seeing shapes everywhere. I had multiple epiphanies while drawing and painting since then, sometimes entering a nirvana state of mind. For me, that dream was a portal, where it all started. Now my passion for art is immense; I write poetry and prose, paint whenever I can, and most recently, making music seems interesting. Hope you enjoy my art. What do you see?
